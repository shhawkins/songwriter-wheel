Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Songwriter Wheel ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using the Songwriter Wheel application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or sign in using Google or email authentication, we may collect:

  • Email address – used to identify your account and allow you to sign in
  • Profile information – such as your name and profile picture, as provided by Google during authentication

User-Generated Content

We store the data you explicitly create within the Service, including:

  • Songs, compositions, and related metadata
  • Custom instrument settings, audio samples, and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Device and browser type
  • Operating system
  • Timestamps and usage activity

This information is used solely for security, performance monitoring, and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only as necessary to operate and improve the Service, including:

  • Authenticating users and maintaining active sessions
  • Storing and syncing your content across devices
  • Improving application performance, stability, and user experience
  • Maintaining security and preventing abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We process your information based on your consent and our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party cloud database and authentication provider. Supabase applies industry-standard security practices to protect stored data.

Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth, which securely integrates with Google OAuth and email-based authentication.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service relies on the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth – used for authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic public profile information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the Service. If you delete your account, all associated personal data and user-generated content are permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

You may manage your data directly within the application:

  • Access and correction – view and edit your saved songs and settings
  • Deletion – permanently delete your account and all associated data using the in-app "Delete Account" option

7. Children's Privacy

Songwriter Wheel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
